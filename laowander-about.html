Our story

Built for people who want to really see Laos.

Not the highlights reel. Not the Instagram spots. The real thing — the slow mornings, the temple silence, the food that nobody told you about.

Why LaoWander exists

Laos is the most underrated country in Southeast Asia.

Most travel sites treat Laos as a footnote — two days in Luang Prabang sandwiched between Thailand and Vietnam. They miss the point entirely. Laos is a destination that rewards the people who slow down, go off-route, and pay attention.

LaoWander was built to be the resource we wished existed when we first arrived. Not a list of the same ten things everyone says to do. Not filler content optimised for search engines. An honest, practical, genuinely useful guide — written by people who care about the country.

"Laos doesn't try to impress you. It just is — quietly, unhurriedly, beautifully. That's exactly why it gets under your skin."

Every hotel recommendation has been vetted. Every restaurant has been eaten at. Every itinerary has been walked. The budget numbers are real. The phrasebook works. The map pins are accurate to the metre.

What we stand for
🔍
Honest
We say when something isn't worth it. We include budget options alongside luxury ones. We don't inflate ratings because a hotel paid for placement.
🗺️
Independent
LaoWander is not owned by a travel conglomerate. No corporate mandates, no paid-for rankings, no agenda other than helping you have a better trip.
🌿
Responsible
We highlight locally-owned businesses, promote respectful tourism, and never recommend anything that we believe harms Lao communities or the environment.
📍
Accurate
Prices are updated regularly. Maps use real GPS coordinates. Opening hours are verified. We'd rather admit we don't know something than make something up.
🤝
Accessible
A backpacker on $25/day and a couple spending $500/day both deserve good advice. LaoWander covers every budget without favouring either.
💛
Made with love
That sounds like a cliché, but it's true. Every page reflects a genuine care for Laos and a genuine desire for you to have an extraordinary time there.

Transparency — how we make money
🏨
Affiliate commissions: When you click a "Book on Booking.com" button and complete a stay, we earn a small commission from Booking.com. This doesn't add anything to your price. We only link to hotels we'd genuinely recommend.
🎯
Activity bookings: Some tour and activity links on the Events page are affiliate links to GetYourGuide and Viator. Same principle — no extra cost to you, small commission to us.
🚫
What we don't do: We don't accept payment for hotel, restaurant, or destination placement. We don't have sponsored posts. We don't inflate scores or write dishonest reviews because someone paid us.
💬
Reader reviews: The reviews on hotel and restaurant cards are submitted by real travellers. We don't write fake reviews or inflate ratings.
