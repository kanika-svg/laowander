What affects your budget

The honest breakdown

Laos is one of Southeast Asia's most affordable destinations — here's what your money actually gets you.

🏨 Accommodation

Budget ($8–25): Fan room in a guesthouse, shared bathroom, basic WiFi. Perfectly comfortable.

Mid-range ($40–100): Air-con boutique hotel, private bathroom, pool access, breakfast sometimes included.

Luxury ($200–700): Rosewood, Amantaka, or Aman — world-class by any standard.

🍜 Food & Drink

Street food ($2–5/meal): Khao Jee baguettes, noodle soup, grilled skewers — genuinely delicious.

Local restaurants ($5–12): Sit-down meals with full Lao dishes, Beer Lao included.

Fine dining ($20–60): Tamarind, MakPhet, Rosewood restaurant — tasting menus and riverside settings.

🚌 Getting Around

Laos-China Railway: Vientiane → Luang Prabang for $8. Fastest and most comfortable option.

Local buses ($3–15): Slow but cheap between major cities. Night buses available.

Slow boat ($25–35): 2-day Mekong journey from the Thai border to Luang Prabang. An experience in itself.
