For First-Time Visitors

Know Before You Go

Practical guidance to make your trip seamless and respectful.

💰

Currency & Payments

The Lao Kip (LAK) is official. USD and Thai Baht are widely accepted. ATMs in cities — carry cash in rural areas. Tipping appreciated but not mandatory.

🛂

Visas & Entry

Most nationalities get Visa on Arrival (30 days) at major crossings and airports — around $30–35 USD. ASEAN members often enter visa-free.

🧘

Temple Etiquette

Cover knees and shoulders. Remove shoes at entrances. Never touch monks. Walk clockwise around stupas. Speak softly in sacred spaces.

🌡️

Best Time to Visit

November–February is cool and dry — ideal. March–May is hot. June–October is rainy but lush. Lao New Year in April is unforgettable.

🛺

Getting Around

Tuk-tuks in cities. Scooters for flexibility (valid licence required). Mekong slow boats are a highlight. Domestic flights link major towns.

🍛

Food & Water

Try laap, khao niao, and tam mak houng. Drink bottled or filtered water only. Street food is safe at busy stalls. Sabaidee! 🙏
